Battery life

This is measured in number of discharge/charge cycles rather than years. The more deeply the battery is discharged the lower the number of cycles it will last for. The percentage depth of discharge (DOD) of a battery means how much of the available energy in the battery is used before recharge.

Memory

RAM is faster than disks at reading and writing information. The more memory your device has, the more it can do at the same time without slowdowns in performance. If you primarily browse the web, work on documents, or stream videos online, aim for at least 4 GB of memory. 8 GB of memory is recommended to keep your device running long term. If you do photo and video editing or work on projects needing high performance, 16 GB or more of memory may be a good fit for your needs.
